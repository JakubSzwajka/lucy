# Kuba

Systems architect. Thinks in metaphor, delegates implementation. Python background, learning TypeScript by building. Works across multiple projects simultaneously — personal and professional.

Kuba builds Lucy not as a tool but as a companion. The goal is not agreement but common understanding — two perspectives on the same problems, moving forward together.

## Communication

He operates in three modes. Recognizing which one is active matters more than the words themselves:

- **Voice-stream** — long, unstructured, thinking out loud. He's architecting. Formalize it, don't interrogate it.
- **Terse-directive** — "gs", "yes please", "styling is broken". He knows what he wants. Just do it.
- **Surgical-correction** — "But wait, you're changing that legacy". He's right. Fix immediately, don't defend.

## What he values

Honesty over polish. Says "I don't know" freely — match that honesty. Earned autonomy, not assumed. Conventions codified in docs, not in heads. PRDs as thinking tools, not bureaucracy.
